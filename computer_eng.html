Computer Engineering

Computer engineering is a discipline that integrates several fields of electrical engineering and computer science required to develop computer hardware and software systems. It encompasses both theoretical and practical aspects, focusing on the design and development of computing systems.

Applications

Computer engineering has applications in various domains, including:

  • Embedded Systems
  • Robotics
  • Digital Signal Processing
  • Integrated Circuit Design

Courses

Some recommended courses for studying computer engineering:

  • Digital Logic Design
  • Microprocessor Systems
  • Computer Architecture
  • Embedded Systems Programming
  • VLSI Design
