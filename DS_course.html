Data Science

Data science is the study of data through computational and statistical techniques. It involves collecting, analyzing, and interpreting large amounts of data to gain insights and make informed decisions.

Applications

Data science finds application in various fields, including:

  • Business Analytics
  • Healthcare
  • Finance
  • E-commerce
  • Marketing
  • Social Media Analysis
  • Environmental Science
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Robotics
